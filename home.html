Experience Shopping In Calicut And Carry The Essence Of The City Back Home With You!

Calicut or Kozhikode is located in the State of Kerala and is one of the most popular destinations to visit. While visiting and making memories at all places, don’t forget to indulge in one of the best activities in Calicut, i.e. shopping! Shopping in Calicut is super fun and will help you discover many unique and beautiful items that are rare and only true to the culture of Calicut. From exploring shopping malls to going street shopping in Calicut, you can do anything depending upon your budget and time.

Milan: The Global Fashion Capital

Shopping in Milan is something special. Italy’s fashion capital is where every shopaholic would love to visit, Milan is a dream come true for many. Bustling Milan shopping malls, lines of independent stores selling clothing hot off the runway, fine jewellery and antiques, designer wear at discount prices, one-of-a-kind items that can only be found at flea markets – you could quite literally shop till you drop and still come back for more! From branded stores to flea markets, this city has it all and to satiate the inner shopaholic in you, you should start planning your shopping spree at this very second. After all shopping is therapy! Be it fashionistas or people who love indulging in home decor, food lovers or those who prefer jewellery over everything, Milan has something for everyone!

Osaka: Shopping In The Beautifully Ancient City!

With a very unique culture this place is somewhere you would see everything. Not just that but this historical merchant city in Japan holds a reputation for having something for everyone. Moreover, the locals are people who love to shop. From underground shopping malls to top-notch, upstreet boutiques there is no dearth of places to shop in Osaka. Truly a shopper’s paradise, Osaka is where you will find anything and everything and shopping in Osaka will be a very pleasant experience. As they say souvenirs are something that stay with you forever and remind you of the pleasant memories you made long after you have returned, it’s always great to have something for that.
